    • Uploading Themes

    Access your blog’s settings

    Select Design from the navigation menu on the left of your admin area.

    Upload your theme

    Next click on the green Upload a theme button.

    This will prompt an upload box to open.
    Click inside the upload box to select a theme zip, or drag-and-drop the theme zip into the upload box.

    Click Activate Now to activate the theme.

    The theme is active on the your blog
