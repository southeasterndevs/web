    • Blog Cover

    Select General from the navigation menu on the left of your admin area.

    Then scroll down and at the bottom you will see an option which says Publication cover.
    Click Upload Image to upload your cover.

    After adding the Blog Cover to the theme it will be shown as the main cover

    Post Cover Photo as Blog Cover

    If you add a Cover Photo to the first post though, it will become a theme’s temporary Blog Cover – it applies to the home page, tag page, author page and the subpages (if the post is shown on the subpages on the first position).
